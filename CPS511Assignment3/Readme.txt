F1: brings up the help menu

HELP FUNCTIONS
p: Changes to periscope view
f: Fires a torpedo
q: lowers the speed of the submarine
e: raises the speed of the submarine
w: raises the submarine
s: lowers the submarine
a: turns the submarine counter-clockwise
d: turns the submarine clockwise
Up Arrow: raises the periscope view
Down Arrow: lowers the periscope view
Spacebar: starts and stops moving the submarine forward
